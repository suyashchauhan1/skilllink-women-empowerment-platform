# SkillLink

SkillLink is an AI-powered women empowerment platform designed to help rural and local women identify their skills and connect with work opportunities within their communities.

The platform allows women to describe the work they know in any language, and the AI intelligently identifies and converts their experience into recognized professional skills. Local employers and community members can then discover and hire them directly through the platform.

SkillLink aims to reduce barriers to employment, increase financial independence, and support women upliftment through technology and AI.

---

# Vision

Millions of women possess valuable skills but are unable to access work opportunities because of:

- language barriers
- lack of formal education
- inability to describe skills professionally
- absence of digital exposure
- disconnected local job systems

SkillLink bridges this gap by transforming informal experience into recognized employable skills and connecting women with local work opportunities.

> From hidden talent to meaningful work.

---

# Overview

SkillLink is built around a simple idea:

Women should not need technical knowledge, resumes, or English fluency to access opportunities.

The platform enables users to:

- describe their work naturally in any language
- get AI-generated skill identification
- create professional skill profiles
- connect with local employers
- apply for work opportunities
- become financially independent

At the same time, local people and businesses who need workers can post jobs directly on the platform.

---

#  Core Features

## AI-Based Skill Identification

Women can describe their work naturally, for example:

```text
"I stitch clothes and help in embroidery work."
```

or in their native language.

The AI analyzes the input and identifies professional skills such as:

- Tailoring
- Embroidery
- Textile Work
- Handcraft Skills

This removes the need for technical vocabulary or formal resumes.

---

## Multilingual Support

The platform supports input in multiple languages to make the system accessible for rural and local communities.

Women can describe their experience comfortably in their native language.

---

## Women Skill Profiles

Users can create profiles containing:

- identified skills
- work experience
- availability
- location
- contact information

This helps create a digital identity for women workers.

---

## Local Job Opportunities

Local employers, households, and businesses can:

- post work opportunities
- hire nearby workers
- connect directly with skilled women

Examples:

- tailoring work
- home tutoring
- stitching
- cooking services
- handicrafts
- local business assistance

---

## Community-Based Hiring

SkillLink focuses on local community employment instead of large corporate hiring systems.

This makes:
- hiring easier
- communication faster
- opportunities more accessible

especially for women in rural areas.

---

## Accessibility & Simplicity

The platform is designed to be:

- beginner-friendly
- mobile accessible
- easy to use
- low-literacy friendly

# How It Works

## Step 1 — Describe Work

The user explains the type of work they know in any language.

Example:

```text
"I help in farming and make handmade baskets."
```

---

## Step 2 — AI Skill Detection

The AI processes the input and identifies skills such as:

- Agriculture Assistance
- Basket Weaving
- Handicrafts

---

## Step 3 — Profile Creation

The platform creates a structured skill profile.

---

## Step 4 — Local Hiring

Employers nearby can:
- search for workers
- view profiles
- contact women directly
- offer work opportunities
